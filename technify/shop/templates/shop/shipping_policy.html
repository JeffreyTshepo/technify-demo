Shipping Information

At Technify, we strive to deliver your orders as quickly and efficiently as possible. Please review our shipping policy below.

📦 Shipping Methods & Delivery Times

Standard Delivery

  • Cost: R100 (FREE on orders over R500)
  • Delivery Time: 1-4 weeks
  • Coverage: All major cities in South Africa

🌍 Delivery Areas

We currently deliver within the Republic of South Africa only. Customers must provide a valid physical address and accurate contact details at checkout.

📅 Processing & Delivery Time

  • Orders are processed within 1-4 business days after payment confirmation
  • You'll receive a confirmation email with tracking details once shipped
  • Delivery typically takes 1-4 weeks from order confirmation
  • Weekend orders are processed on the next business day

💰 Free Shipping Promotion

🎉 Get FREE Shipping!

Orders over R500 qualify for FREE delivery across South Africa.

Discount automatically applied at checkout.

📍 Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you'll receive:

  • Email confirmation with tracking number
  • Estimated delivery date
  • Ability to track your order in real-time

📦 Receiving Your Order

  • A responsible person must be available at the delivery address
  • You may be required to sign for delivery
  • Inspect items upon delivery for any visible damage
  • If no one is available, courier will leave a collection notice

🏪 Collection Option

In certain circumstances, you may collect your order. Where collection is approved:

  • No delivery fee will apply
  • Collection from: nearest pickup point or lockers near you
  • Orders available for 7 days after notification
