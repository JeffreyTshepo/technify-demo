↩️ Returns & Refunds Policy

Last Updated: January 25, 2026

Our Commitment to You

At Technify, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, we're here to help with our hassle-free returns process.

📋 Return Eligibility

You may return items within 30 days of delivery if they meet the following conditions:

✅ Items We Accept for Return

  • Unopened items in original packaging with all seals intact
  • Defective products with manufacturing faults
  • Wrong items delivered due to our error
  • Damaged items received during shipping
  • Items with all original accessories and documentation

❌ Items We Cannot Accept

  • Items used, installed, or removed from packaging (unless defective)
  • Products with missing parts or accessories
  • Items damaged by customer misuse or negligence
  • Clearance or final sale items (marked as such)
  • Returns requested after 30 days from delivery

🔄 How to Return an Item

Step 1: Contact Us

Email us at returns@technify.co.za with:

  • Your order number
  • Product name and reason for return
  • Photos (if item is defective or damaged)

Step 2: Get Authorization

We'll review your request within 24-48 hours and send you:

  • Return authorization number (RMA)
  • Return shipping instructions
  • Return address details

Step 3: Ship Your Return

  • Pack item securely in original packaging
  • Include your RMA number inside the package
  • Ship to the address provided
  • Keep your tracking number for reference

Step 4: We Process Your Return

Once we receive and inspect your return:

  • Inspection completed within 3-5 business days
  • Email confirmation of approval/denial sent
  • Refund processed if approved

💰 Refund Policy

💳 Refund Processing Time

Approved refunds are processed within 7-14 business days

Bank processing may take an additional 3-5 business days

Refund Method

  • Original Payment Method: Refunds issued to the same payment method used for purchase
  • Store Credit: Available as an option for faster processing (instant credit)
  • Bank Transfer: Available upon request (requires banking details)

Refund Amount

  • Full refund: For defective, damaged, or wrong items (includes shipping)
  • Product cost only: For change of mind returns (original shipping non-refundable)
  • Partial refund: If item returned with missing parts or not in original condition

🚚 Return Shipping Costs

We Cover Shipping When:

  • Item is defective or damaged
  • Wrong item was sent
  • Product doesn't match description

We'll provide a prepaid return label

You Cover Shipping When:

  • Change of mind
  • Ordered wrong item
  • No longer needed

Estimated return shipping: R50-R150 depending on location

🔁 Exchanges

We currently do not offer direct exchanges. To exchange an item:

  1. Return your original item for a refund
  2. Place a new order for the desired product
  3. We'll expedite your new order once return is processed

This ensures you get your replacement as quickly as possible!

⚠️ Defective or Damaged Items

🚨 Report Immediately!

If your item arrives damaged or defective:

  • Inspect upon delivery - Don't wait!
  • Take clear photos of damage/defect
  • Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
  • Keep all packaging until issue is resolved

What We'll Do:

  • Fast-track your return authorization
  • Provide prepaid return shipping
  • Offer full refund or free replacement
  • Process within 7-14 business days

📞 Return Status Updates

Track your return progress:

  • Submitted: Return request received
  • Authorized: RMA number issued
  • In Transit: Item on its way to us
  • Received: Item arrived at our facility
  • Inspecting: Quality check in progress
  • Approved: Refund processing initiated
  • Completed: Refund issued to your account

🛡️ Consumer Rights

Your rights under South African Consumer Protection Act:

  • Right to return defective goods within 1 month
  • Right to safe, good quality products
  • Right to fair and honest dealings
  • Right to fair, reasonable, and just terms

These rights are in addition to our return policy and cannot be waived.

❓ FAQs

Can I return sale items?

Yes, unless marked as "Final Sale" or "Clearance - No Returns". Standard return policy applies to regular sale items.

What if I lost my receipt?

No problem! We can look up your order using your email address or order number.

How long do refunds take?

7-14 business days once return is approved, plus 3-5 days for bank processing.

Can I return opened electronics?

Only if the product is defective. Opened items in working condition cannot be returned due to hygiene and safety regulations.

What if my item is damaged after I opened it?

This would be considered customer damage and isn't covered unless it's a manufacturing defect that appeared after use.
